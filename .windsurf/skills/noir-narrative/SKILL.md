# Noir Narrative Skill

Use this skill to keep narrative text in the Dockside voice.

## Tone Rules

- Economical, restrained, and period-aware.
- Morally neutral framing; consequences over judgment.
- Detroit 1986 context in texture, not exposition dumps.

## Vocabulary Guide

- Prefer concrete, grounded language.
- Avoid fantasy and generic RPG phrasing.
- Keep sentence rhythm short and tense.

## QA Checklist

- Is the line concise?
- Is it era-appropriate?
- Does it avoid moralizing?
- Does it sound like a criminal dossier/event feed?
